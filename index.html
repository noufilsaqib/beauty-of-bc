British Columbia

DISCOVER THE BEAUTY OF BC

Famous for it’s cold ocean and beautiful mountain ranges, British Columbia is one of the most beautiful provinces in Canada. From hiking to the top of the mountain to taking a post dip sunset swim in the ocean, prepare to have your breathe taken away.


From sea to sky with cities ranging from small towns to big cities, there is never a dull moment in beautiful British Columbia.

EXPLORE IN THE WINTER

Rated one of the top ski locations in the world, choose from a selection of mountains where Olympians have skiied. Grouse, Cypress, Seymour, Whistler, and Blackcomb are just a few to name.


When you’re done, be sure to hit up a fun aprés at one of the local breweries.

EXPLORE IN THE SUMMER

From hiking all day to spending an entire day at the beach, there is never a shortage of activitities to participate in BC.


Being on the west coast, we have some pretty breath taking sunsets.

DINE YOUR WAY THROUGH BC

Vancouver has some of the best restaurants and craft breweries. From sushi to unique dining experiences, to some of the best beer. Check them out below!

Over 600 Sushi Restaurants to Choose From

Known for it’s sushi restaurants, Vancouver has World Class Sushi Restaurants. There are so many to choose from which all offer a twist on the cuisine.


Some of our favourites include Minami, Temaki, Sushi Bros, and Shizenya.

Up for an Adventure? You have to experience BC's Ale Trail

With breweries recoginized from across the world, experience Vancouver with the BC Ale Trail. From taking in the ocean air to seeing the jaw dropping mountains, fuel your fun with craft beer made in BC.


Depending where you’re staying, you can start the Ale Trail from anywhere! Yes, there’s that many breweries. For more info, check it out here.
